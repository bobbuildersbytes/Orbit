**See who’s free and find what’s fun. Turn screen time into real time.**

Orbit is a social discovery tool designed to eliminate the difficulty of planning and fight social isolation. By blending real-time location data with an intelligent Spontaneity Engine, Orbit transforms your city into a map of possibilities, helping you move from digital browsing to real-world connection.

**Features**
- The Space Stage: A high-fidelity, interactive interface built with glassmorphic design principles to visualize your social circle.
- Spontaneity Engine: AI-driven logic that recognizes when friends are nearby and suggests instant "Missions" like coffee, arcade trips, or dinner.
- Real-Time Presence: See at a glance who is available to hang out without the back-and-forth of group chats.
- Fleet Dynamics: A relational identity system where your profile is shaped by your actions and the communities you build.

**Tech Stack**
Frontend
- HTML & CSS: For complex animations and layered visual effects.
- JavaScript: Powering the interactive UI and real-time updates.

Backend
- Node.js: Scalable server-side logic.
- MongoDB: Flexible NoSQL database for managing user states and friendships.
- Backboard API: Core data structure management.
- Nodemailer: Secure communication and automated notifications.

Analytics
- Amplitude: Tracking engagement to refine AI-driven activity suggestions.

In Orbit, we believe identity is built on your presence. We move away from curated digital personas and focus on how you show up for the people in your life. Your identity is defined by your reliability and the experiences you share, turning your social life into a collaborative journey.
